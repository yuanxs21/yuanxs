About

I am a 5th-year Ph.D. candidate at Jilin University, advised by Prof. Renchu Guan. I work on understanding and improving the reasoning capabilities of large language models, with recent emphasis on inference-time optimization, supervised fine-tuning, and agentic systems.

Earlier, my research focused on causal relation extraction from open-domain texts and controllable text generation. I have been a research intern at DAMO Academy (City Brain Lab), Ant Group, and currently at Tongyi Lab, Alibaba. Now, I am working on LLM agent systems, contributing to the development of QwenPaw (… stars on GitHub).
